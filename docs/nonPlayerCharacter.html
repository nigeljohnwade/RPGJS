Non-Player Character

The playerCharacter object is used for each of the players in the game. It holds the defined statistics and calculates the players abilities and derived properties from these statistics. In addition to deriving abilities from statistics the object holds links to an inventory object which may hold items that also affect the players abilities

Statistics

Statistics are defined with the character at the time of construction. They are passed in with the constructor or else the constructor sets them to default values

Melee

The melee statistic determines the player character's general effectiveness with close quarters combat weapons. Weapon specific ability modifiers are only gained through inventory items.

See Combat for more details

Ranged

The ranged statistic determines the player character's general effectiveness with combat weapons that deal damage from afar. For these weapons range is important and may have an affect on the weapon's damage and/­or number of hits inflicted Weapon specific ability modifiers are only gained through inventory items.

See Combat for more details

Special

The special statistic determines the player character's general effectiveness with whatever this may be used to represent in the game universe. It may be magic, mental powers or whatever else is pertinent to the type of game or indeed the type of character. Specific ability modifiers are only gained through inventory items.

See Combat for more details
